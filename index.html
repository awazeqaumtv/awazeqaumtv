Privacy Policy for awazeqaumtv.com

This privacy policy is designed to inform users about how their ‘Personally Identifiable Information’ (PII) is collected and used on our website. PII, as defined by privacy laws and information security practices, includes information that can be used alone or in conjunction with other data to identify, contact, or locate a specific individual. Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand how we gather, use, protect, and handle your PII on awazeqaumtv.com.

What personal information do we collect from users of our website or app?

When registering or making a purchase on our site, you may be asked to provide your name, email address, phone number, or other details to enhance your experience.

When do we collect information?

We collect information when you register on our site, subscribe to our newsletter, participate in a survey, or enter information on our website.

How do we use your information?

The information we collect may be used in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience and deliver the content or product offerings that are most relevant to you.
  • To improve our website and provide better service to you.
  • To respond to your customer service requests more efficiently.
  • To manage contests, promotions, surveys, or other site features.
  • To send periodic emails about your orders or other products and services.

How do we protect your information?

Our website undergoes regular security checks to identify vulnerabilities and ensure a secure browsing experience. We also perform regular malware scans. Your personal information is stored behind secured networks and is accessible only by a limited number of people with special access rights who are required to maintain the confidentiality of the data. Any sensitive information, such as credit card data, is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. We implement various security measures when you enter, submit, or access your information to protect your personal data.

All transactions are processed through a gateway provider and are not stored or processed on our servers.

Do we use ‘cookies’?

Yes, we use cookies. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device through your web browser (if permitted) to allow the site to recognize your browser and remember specific information. We use cookies to:

  • Understand and save user preferences for future visits.
  • Track advertisements.
  • Compile aggregate data about site traffic and interactions to improve future user experiences.

You can choose to have your computer warn you when a cookie is being sent or turn off all cookies through your browser settings. Note that some website features may not function correctly if you disable cookies.

COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act)

We do not knowingly market to children under the age of 13. The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) gives parents control over the collection of personal information from children under 13 years old.

CAN-SPAM Act

The CAN-SPAM Act sets the rules for commercial emails, requires transparency in email communications, and gives recipients the right to stop emails from being sent to them. We collect your email address to send information related to orders and other services.

In compliance with CAN-SPAM, if you would like to unsubscribe from our emails, you can do so by contacting us and we will promptly remove you from all correspondence.
